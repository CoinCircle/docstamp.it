Do I need Ether to use docstamp.it? Yes Do you store my documents? No. We do not store the contents of your document anywhere. Are the contents of my documents safe? Yes, We only store the cryptographic hash of your documents. The hash is calculated on your computer and serves as a fingerprint for your document. It is impossible to reconstruct your document from this hash. Why should I use docstamp.it? docstamp.it is implemented using a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain. When you record a document using docstamp.it, you are guaranteed that this record is immutable. This information cannot be lost, it cannot be modified even by governments and you can always verify this information even if docstamp.it is down Can I use docstamp.it for contracts? Yes.
